### Description (≤4000)
```
LG webOS Remote turns your iPhone into a fast, native remote control for your LG smart TV. It talks directly to your TV over your local Wi-Fi network — no account, no sign-up, no ads, and no data ever leaves your home network.

Built with a clean, dark, glass-style interface that feels right at home on iOS.

FEATURES

• Auto-discovery — automatically finds LG webOS TVs on your network. Just tap to connect.
• Power on & off — wake your TV from standby with Wake-on-LAN, and power it back off from the app.
• Volume control — real-time on-screen volume readout that stays in sync with your TV.
• Mute — one-tap mute and unmute.
• Channels — quick channel up and down.
• Input switching — jump between HDMI, the live TV tuner, and your other connected inputs.
• D-pad navigation — full directional pad with OK, Back, Home, and Menu for navigating apps and menus.
• Haptic feedback — every button press feels tactile and responsive.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

The app communicates only with your TV, only on your local network. It does not collect analytics, it has no trackers, and it requires no account. Your TV's address and pairing key are stored on your device.

HOW TO SET UP

1. Make sure your iPhone and LG TV are on the same Wi-Fi network.
2. Open the app — it scans your network and lists the LG TVs it finds.
3. Tap your TV, then accept the pairing prompt that appears on the TV screen.
4. That's it. The pairing key is saved, so future connections are instant.

For Wake-on-LAN (turning the TV on while it's fully off), add your TV's MAC address once in Settings — you'll find it in your TV's network settings.

REQUIREMENTS

• An LG TV running webOS (2014 models or newer).
• iPhone and TV connected to the same local network.
• "LG Connect Apps" / "Mobile TV On" enabled in your TV's network settings for best results, including Wake-on-LAN.

LG webOS Remote is an independent app and is not affiliated with, authorized, endorsed by, or in any way officially connected to LG Electronics. "LG" and "webOS" are trademarks of their respective owners and are used here only to describe the TVs this app is compatible with.
```
