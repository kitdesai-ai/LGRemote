Privacy Policy

LG webOS Remote · Last updated June 23, 2026

We don't collect any data.

LG webOS Remote ("the app") is designed to control your LG webOS television over your local network. Your privacy is built in: the app does not collect, store on our servers, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

Information we collect

None. The app has no analytics, no advertising, no third-party trackers, and no user accounts. We never see or receive any data from your use of the app.

Information stored on your device

To connect to your TV, the app saves the following locally on your iPhone only (in the app's local storage):

  • Your TV's IP address
  • Your TV's MAC address (used for Wake-on-LAN), if you enter it
  • The pairing key your TV issues when you accept the connection

This information never leaves your device. Deleting the app removes it.

Network access

The app communicates only with your TV, only on your local Wi-Fi network, using LG's SSAP protocol over a WebSocket connection. It makes no connections to any servers operated by us or by third parties.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
